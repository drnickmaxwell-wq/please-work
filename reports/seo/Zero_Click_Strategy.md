# Zero-Click & AI Overview Strategy

## Objectives
Capture AI Overview, People Also Ask, and featured snippet space through concise, structured, and schema-aligned content.

---

### 1. Home
**Blocks:**  
- Micro-FAQ rail (2-4 top questions)  
- Mini HowTo (“How to book an e-consult”)  
**Schema pairing:** FAQPage + HowTo  
**Copy pattern:** direct, 2-sentence answers using plain English; no marketing filler.  
**Accessibility:** aria-expanded toggles; keyboard-focus order maintained.

---

### 2. Treatments
**Blocks:**  
- “How It Works” 4-step HowTo with schema  
- Pricing band with Offer markup  
- Speakable cost and duration headings  
**Zero-click angle:** appear for “how long do veneers last”, “cost of implants UK” queries.  
**Schema pairing:** Service + HowTo + FAQPage  
**Guard-safe copy:** avoids jargon; includes disclaimer about individual variation.  

---

### 3. Technology
**Blocks:**  
- Definition snippets (“What is CBCT?”)  
- Short comparisons (traditional vs digital impressions)  
**Schema pairing:** Product + TechArticle + FAQPage  
**Copy pattern:** 40-60 word explainers optimised for voice search.  
**Accessibility:** Captioned videos; alt text for devices.

---

### 4. About
**Blocks:**  
- Team bio summaries eligible for “who is Dr Nick Maxwell” search.  
- Mission paragraph crafted for “about St Mary’s House Dental.”  
**Schema pairing:** Organization + Person  
**Guard-safe copy:** emphasises credentials, ethics, and affiliations.

---

### 5. Patient Stories
**Blocks:**  
- Case grids with concise captions (“Veneers for chipped teeth – 2 weeks”).  
- Review-style narrative using Review schema.  
**Zero-click aim:** appear under “veneers before and after Shoreham”.  
**Accessibility:** Captions readable at zoom 200%; descriptive alt text.

---

### 6. AI Smile Quiz
**Blocks:**  
- HowTo snippet of steps (consent → upload → result).  
- FAQ on photo storage and AI explanation.  
**Schema pairing:** HowTo + SoftwareApplication + FAQPage  
**Accessibility:** Step focus order; reduced motion for PRM.

---

### 7. Portal
**Blocks:**  
- HowTo (“How to log in to the patient portal”).  
- FAQ about security and app use.  
**Schema pairing:** SoftwareApplication + FAQPage  

---

### 8. Contact
**Blocks:**  
- FAQ with business hours and emergency contacts.  
- LocalBusiness schema for map visibility.  
**Zero-click aim:** “dentist near me open now” and local map pack enrichment.  
**Accessibility:** ARIA labels for phone/email; keyboard-accessible map.

---

### 9. Blog
**Blocks:**  
- FAQ at article end; summarised HowTo if relevant.  
**Schema pairing:** BlogPosting + FAQPage  
**Guard-safe pattern:** factual intro paragraphs answering query directly before expansion.
